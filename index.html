FAQ

Quick answers

The questions buyers typically ask before sending an RFQ.

What files can I send?

DXF, DWG, STEP, PDF, or ZIP. If you’re not sure, send what you have — we’ll tell you quickly if it’s a fit.

How fast do you quote?

Typically within 36 hours. If it’s urgent, call it out in the notes or email us directly.

Do you support finishing and hardware?

Yes — hardware insertion, finishing, and packaging/kitting are available and confirmed in your quote.

What kind of work is a great fit?

Laser-cut parts, formed sheet metal, plates/panels, brackets, guards, and small assemblies — especially short-run or quick-turn work.
