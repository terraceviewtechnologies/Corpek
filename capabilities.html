Core capabilities

These are the common lanes we support. If your job doesn’t fit neatly into a box, send it anyway — we’ll tell you quickly if it’s a fit.

Precision Cutting

Laser cutting for prototypes through production runs. Clean edges, consistent results, and repeatable output.

Typical parts: plates, panels, brackets, guards, flat patterns.

Fabrication + Forming

Bending, assemblies, and weldment execution/coordination. Quoted per job based on scope and critical features.

Tip: call out cosmetic faces + tight bend requirements.

Secondary Ops

Tapping, countersinks/counterbores, deburr, and simple machining features supported (quoted per job).

Built for “make it hardware-ready” requirements.

Production Control

Revision verification, requirement checks, and quality handling so parts don’t move forward on assumptions.

The goal: clean parts the first run.

DFM Review

We flag risks early — hole-to-edge, thin walls, tight bends, hardware fit, tolerance stack-up — before production.

If something needs clarification, we ask before release.

Delivery + Handling

Standard and expedited options available. Packaging, labeling, and kitting can be included when needed

Timing confirmed in your quote based on routing + finishing.
