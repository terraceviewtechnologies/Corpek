About Us

Corpek Manufacturing began as a small engineering consulting firm focused on improving manufacturing processes, cleaning up drawings, and helping local shops solve day‑to‑day production challenges. Over time, we saw the same pattern from both sides of the industry. Customers struggled to find the right place to take their parts, and shops were stretched thin trying to manage unclear prints, shifting requirements, and constant communication.

That experience pushed us to expand beyond engineering support and step into the operational side of manufacturing. Today, Corpek reviews customer drawings, selects the right production partner, manages communication, and ensures the final parts meet expectations. We also continue to build our own internal capabilities, allowing us to support projects directly when it makes sense.

Our approach is simple. Use practical engineering experience to make manufacturing easier. Communicate clearly. Deliver reliably. And treat both customers and shops with the respect their work deserves.

Corpek exists because we’ve lived the challenges on both sides of the industry, and we know there’s a better way to get parts made.

FAQ

Quick answers

The questions buyers typically ask before sending an RFQ.

What files can I send?

DXF, DWG, STEP, PDF, or ZIP. If you’re not sure, send what you have — we’ll tell you quickly if it’s a fit.

How fast do you quote?

Typically within 36 hours. If it’s urgent, call it out in the notes or email us directly.

Do you support finishing and hardware?

Yes — hardware insertion, finishing, and packaging/kitting are available and confirmed in your quote.

What kind of work is a great fit?

Laser-cut parts, formed sheet metal, plates/panels, brackets, guards, and small assemblies — especially short-run or quick-turn work.
