Production-ready services for parts & projects

Projects rarely end at cutting. Whether you need hole operations, bending, or finishing,
we help you get clean, production-ready parts with a controlled workflow and clear expectations.

Typical lead time is 4–6 days for many jobs; timing varies by material, quantity, process routing, and finishing. We confirm schedule in your quote.

Our Services

Sheet metal, plastic, composite, and wood CNC cutting

We cut parts using the method that best fits your material and design requirements. Clean edges, predictable tolerances, and repeatable results.

CNC Routing

Great for acrylics, composites, HDPE/UHMW, and wood where routed edges and large-format cutting shine.

Laser Cutting

Fast, precise sheet cutting for common metals and select plastics. Excellent edge quality for critical parts.

Waterjet Cutting

Ideal for composites and temperature-sensitive materials where you want a clean cut without heat effects.

CNC machining for custom parts

Precision machining for features beyond 2D profiles—milling, drilling, tapping, engraving, and more.

Multiaxis CNC Machining

Full capability milling, tapping/threading, engraving, and secondary processing. Typical lead times are often 4–6 days depending on complexity.

Sheet metal fabrication & hole operations

Add functional details and hardware-ready features—clean, consistent, and production-friendly.

Countersinking

Let flat-head fasteners sit flush to reduce snags and wear.

Dimple Forming

Strengthen material around holes with clean, repeatable dimples.

Hardware Insertion

Press-fit hardware (nuts, studs, standoffs) for strong assemblies.

Tapping

Add clean threads to enable fasteners, mounts, and repeat builds.

Precision bent sheet metal & plastic parts

Parts are rarely flat. We offer bending for common sheet metals and select plastics. Send your flat pattern + bend notes (or a STEP file) and we’ll quote it quickly. Typical lead time is often 4–6 days depending on scope.

Finishing options

Give your parts the durability and look they deserve—protection, cosmetics, and consistency.

Anodizing

Boost corrosion resistance and surface hardness with consistent finishes.

Plating

Improve corrosion resistance and durability for harsh environments.

Powder Coating

A durable protective layer available in multiple colors and textures.

Tumbling

Reduce sharp edges and handling marks for a smoother appearance.
